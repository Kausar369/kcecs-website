Infrastructure

One of our core business sectors

The China–Kazakhstan International Economic Development Center identifies infrastructure development as one of its eight core business sectors. Our scope of services includes, but is not limited to:

  • Real Estate Development: Development and investment in residential, commercial, and industrial park projects.
  • Building Decoration: Interior and exterior design, renovation, and comprehensive project management services.
  • Municipal Infrastructure Construction: Participation in the construction of municipal projects such as roads, bridges, and public facilities.

Dehang Group

Our Subsidiary

Dehang Group is a key subsidiary of the Kazakhstan–China International Economic Development Center, responsible for the implementation and operation of infrastructure-related projects. Originally established as an independent professional enterprise, the Group has now been formally integrated into the Center’s system, focusing on infrastructure investment, construction, and operations. Founded in 1998, Dehang Group follows a people-oriented and integrity-driven management philosophy, supported by established quality, environmental, and occupational health and safety systems. Its core business areas include real estate development, smart city services, logistics and industrial parks, steel structure manufacturing, data ecosystem operations, and global materials supply cooperation.
